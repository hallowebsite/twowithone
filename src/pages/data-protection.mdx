# Data protection

We don't collect any data about visitors of this site. 

## Cookies and similar technologies

We don't use any analytics system, no marketing or ad solutions so we don't
save any data on your device.

If you change the theme from light to dark or the other way around we will
store information about your choice on your device in so called local storage of your
browser, but this information is not sent to our server.

**That is all, enjoy your visit.**
